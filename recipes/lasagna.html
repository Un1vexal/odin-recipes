Sweet Potato Lasagna

Description

This a no noodles verison of lasagna, created for for people looking lose weight but also have a potentially more delicious sweet and savory meal, with better marcos for people trying to lose weight without giving up their favorites foods. It makes small tweaks to some ingredients that at first chance may not taste too good, but after you give this recipe a shot, I promise you won't go back to any old lasagna

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lb lean ground turkey
  • 400g sweet potato (100g each lasagna, thinly sliced)
  • 1 egg white
  • 1/2 cup low fat cottage cheese
  • 1/2 cup reduced fat mozzarella
  • 2 vine tomatoes
  • organic tomato sauce (no salt)
  • 1/3 cup mushrooms
  • 1/3 cup red onions
  • 1 tbsp garlic paste
  • 1/2 cup chopped cilantro (use as desired)
  • Seasonings: 1 tbsp Italian seasoning, sea salt, pepper

Steps

  1. Set oven to 375F
  2. Slice sweet potatos using a sharo knife and separately chop up tomatoes, onions and mushrooms.
  3. Mix cottage cheese with egg white and set aside
  4. Saute red onions with garlic paste, then add meat, tomatoes, cilantro, mushrooms, tomato sauce, Italian seasoning.
  5. Build lasagna, Add sweet potato slices, meat sauce, cottage cheese and then repeat. Top with mozzarella
  6. Cover with aluminum foil and bake for 45 minutes at 375F
